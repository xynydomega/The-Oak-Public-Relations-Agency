# Project Overview: Strategic Communication Website

This project is a high-polish, professional web application for a strategic communication consultancy. It is built with React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, and Framer Motion for smooth animations.

## Page Descriptions

### 1. Home (`/src/pages/Home.tsx`)
The primary entry point of the application. It establishes the brand's visual identity and provides a high-level overview of the consultancy's value proposition.
- **Hero Section**: A bold, high-contrast introduction with elegant typography.
- **Service Overview**: Brief introductions to core offerings.
- **Call to Action**: Directing users to engage with the firm.

### 2. About (`/src/pages/About.tsx`)
Detailing the history, mission, and philosophy of the organisation.
- **Core Values**: Defining the principles that guide the consultancy's work.
- **The Philosophy of the Oak**: A section dedicated to the metaphor of enduring reputation through deep roots and steady growth.
- **Team/Leadership**: (If applicable) Information about the experts behind the strategies.

### 3. Our Approach (`/src/pages/OurApproach.tsx`)
A deep dive into the consultancy's methodology and service categories.
- **Methodology**: "Purpose before process" – emphasizing strategic understanding over tactical execution.
- **The Roadmap**: A six-phase progression (Discover, Analyse, Strategise, Execute, Measure, Evolve) that outlines the client journey.
- **Service Categories**: Detailed cards describing Strategic Communication, PR, Reputation Management, Crisis Comm, Leadership Comm, and Public Engagement.
- **How It Works**: Highlighting the collaborative, tailored, and measured nature of their partnerships.

### 4. Insights (`/src/pages/Insights.tsx`)
A thought leadership hub providing articles, case studies, and industry analysis.
- **Resource Grid**: A filterable or categorised list of insights.
- **Content Previews**: Engaging cards that lead to detailed deep-dives.

### 5. Contact (`/src/pages/Contact.tsx`)
The final destination for potential clients ready to "start the conversation."
- **Inquiry Form**: A clean, accessible form for lead generation.
- **Direct Details**: Providing office locations or direct contact methods.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: React 18+ with Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS for a refined, custom UI.
- **Animations**: Framer Motion for sophisticated entrance and interaction effects.
- **Icons**: Lucide-react for clean, consistent iconography.
- **Typography**: Inter (Sans) and Lora (Serif) for a professional, editorial feel.
